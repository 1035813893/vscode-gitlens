The Visual File History allows you to clearly see the history of a file, including: when changes were made, how large they were, and who made them. Authors who have contributed changes to a file are on one axis, and dates on the other. Color-coded dots represent points in time where that author made changes to the file, and vertical lines represent the magnitude of the change in two colors: green for lines added, and red for lines removed.
